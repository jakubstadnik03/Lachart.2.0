New · Training planning

From a blank day to a structured session in minutes.

LaChart is built for coaches and self-coached athletes who plan intervals, not just log them. Build a workout, schedule it on the calendar, then compare plan vs. actual after the ride.

Workout builder
Workout planner

Build structured sessions down to the rep

Stack warm-up, work blocks, recoveries and cool-down. Set targets by power, heart rate or pace from your lactate zones. The plan renders as a live power profile and rolls up total load before you schedule it.

  • Interval blocks — 4×(3×2′ / 1′ off) in a few clicks
  • Quick templates — VO2max, threshold, sweet spot, sprint
  • Save & reuse — build once, drop onto any week
  • Assign to athletes — coaches send the plan with one click
Calendar · week 12
Calendar

Plan your season on one page

Drag planned workouts onto any day, colour them by load type, and see planned vs. completed side-by-side. Coaches get a multi-athlete weekly view without switching accounts.

  • Week and month views with TSS / load totals
  • Drag & drop sessions between days
  • Multi-athlete view for coaches
Session detail
Lactate per interval

Log lactate against a specific interval

A finger-prick after every rep? Log it straight onto the interval where you measured. The session shows zones, power, heart rate and lactate markers on a single timeline — then compare against what you planned.

  • Lactate-by-interval entry for each rep
  • Session visual with work blocks and recovery interleaved
  • Plan vs. actual — prescribed vs. ridden
New · Lactate tests

Smarter analysis. And finally — your curve, your call.

We rewrote the algorithm behind LT1 and LT2. The result: a cleaner fit, more stable thresholds, and a PDF report you'll happily send to athletes and colleagues.

New OBLA 2.0 model
Curve analysis

Better fit, clearer thresholds

A new log-log and polynomial fit gives stable results even on noisy tests. LT1 and LT2 are computed from multiple methods in parallel — pick the one that fits your protocol.

  • Multiple methods side by side — Dmax, log-log, fixed 2.0/4.0 mmol/L
  • Stage-by-stage values highlighted on hover
  • Compare with your last test right in the chart
Manual mode
Your call

Override LT1, LT2 and your zones

Coaches know best. Generated values can now be dragged on the curve, nudged by a few watts, or overwritten by hand — zones recompute instantly and save back to the athlete profile.

  • Drag & type — drag a point on the chart or type a value
  • Custom zones — 5, 7 or your own split
  • Auto-rollback — revert to the generated value anytime
Export

A PDF report that looks like it came from a lab

Redesigned layout, readable typography, stage-by-stage table, and a curve with thresholds and your zones drawn in. Header with your club or lab logo, plus a one-page summary for the athlete.

  • Custom logo and colors in the header
  • Athlete and coach versions in one click
  • Stage-by-stage table with lactate, power and heart rate
